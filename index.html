Muscle Cars

Muscle cars are high-performance, American-made vehicles with powerful V8 engines, aggressive styling, and rear-wheel drive, designed for speed and straight-line acceleration, popular from the 1960s to today.

Dodge Challenger Hellcat

The Dodge Challenger Hellcat is a high-performance muscle car with a supercharged 6.2L V8, producing over 700 horsepower. It features aggressive styling, rear-wheel drive, and blistering acceleration for ultimate power.
